\begin{abstract}
\textit 

R2P is a mobile application for the purpose of recruiting people interested in participating in the studies of the company ICF.
The application's main function is to inform registered users who are new company studies. If the user wants to apply, he can contact the company through the information provided in the application.
The application also aims to disseminate the clinical study that is done by the ICF company. This clinical study is the fundamental basis for the new drug to be approved by ANVISA. In this way, R2P was designed and implemented in the technological bases for mobile devices.
The application has a personal registration screen, a personal data change screen, a message view screen for studies and a contact screen with the ICF company.

\end{abstract}
